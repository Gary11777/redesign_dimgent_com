## **Overview**

**Garand 101** is a **high-resolution fluxgate magnetometer-gradiometer** designed to measure disruptions in the Earth's magnetic field caused by ferromagnetic objects.
It provides reliable detection of metals such as **iron, steel, and nickel**.
The device is designed for **one-person operation**, being **lightweight**, **user-friendly**, **reliable**, and **low-cost**.
